About

OUR MOTTO IS TO PROVIDE ALL TYPES OF BOOKS FOR ALL

Launched in 2022 bibilophile.com is a popular ebook retailer with a reputation for innovation, integrity and independence. We sell ebooks direct to millions of consumers around the world.
You can read our ebooks online, or download them to a vast array of devices.
bibilophile.com is privately held and run by its founders, life-long booksellers Ramya Sai and Thaswika .
It is the only independent ebook retailer that sells into every country in the world.
We have our own publisher licenses, delivery platforms, and repository of titles.
bibilophile.com hosts millions of unique ebook titles and has millions of members.
In addition to our popular ebook-store, we provide services to book publishers.
Digital Comps is a system that enables publishers to send secure ebooks to reviewers and instructors, called “complimentary copies”.
Ebook Engine is a system that enables publishers to sell ebooks direct to consumers from their own website. And publishers use our Ebook Collections platform to sell collections of their ebooks direct to institutions.
